About Mike

Mike has been tattooing for over 15 years, mastering the art of realism and black & grey work. His passion lies in creating truly unique and breathtaking pieces that tell a story. He is known for his incredible attention to detail, smooth gradients, and ability to bring any vision to life, from intricate portraits to expansive scenic pieces. Mike's studio prides itself on a sterile, comfortable, and welcoming environment for all clients.

He is constantly pushing his artistic boundaries and is a recipient of multiple awards at conventions for his realistic animal and portrait tattoos.
